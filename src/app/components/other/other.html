About Twitch Plays Chess

Welcome to the referendum based public chess session.
With this app you can play chess with strangers over the internet by voting which move should be made next on the board.
This app was made by Pavel Smirnov, student of Helsinki university, as part of a project assignment to implement an app with AngularJS/NodeJS frameworks in 10 days.
Also Twitch has nothing to do with this app. Just a reference to pokeymanz.
